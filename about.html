About UrbanEye AI

UrbanEye AI is a smart city infrastructure monitoring platform designed to help citizens report urban issues such as potholes, damaged roads, broken street lights, garbage overflow, and water leakage quickly and efficiently.

Using AI powered image analysis, the system can detect infrastructure problems and automatically route complaints to the appropriate municipal authorities.

This platform supports smart city initiatives by improving transparency, reducing response time, and enabling collaboration between citizens and government authorities.
